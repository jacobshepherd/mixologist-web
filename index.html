What is it?

The Mixologist is a voice-powered robotic bartender that uses natural language to take your order and serve your favorite mixed drinks.

How does it work?

Using the latest advancements in machine learning and natural language processing, our Google Assistant skill, Mixologist, takes your order through voice and automatically makes your drink, mixing in a dash of fun conversation. Load up your favorite alcohol and mixers into the bottles at the top and watch them light up as pumps precisely pour each ingredient.

What type of liquids can I use?

Pick your favorite alcohols, juices and other mixers, and load up to eight ingredients at a time. Support for carbonated options is coming soon!
