FAQ

Got questions? We’ve got answers. If you have some other questions, feel free to send us an email to hello@mixologist.ai

What is it?

The Mixologist is a voice-powered robotic bartender that uses natural language to take your order and serve your favorite mixed drinks.

How does it work?

The voice is powered by the Google Assistant with help from our custom skill: Mixologist. The Mixologist is loaded with liquids of your choosing at the top and with the integrated pumps it is able to pull the perfect about of each ingredient into your beverage.

What type of liquids can I use?

The current configuration allows support for many types of liquids and a total of eight at a time. We are currently working on a way to support carbonated and pulpy liquids, so stay away from those for now.
